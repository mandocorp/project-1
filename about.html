About Jacob

"who am I and what is my mission?" ~  Jacob Willmott

Jacob Willmott is an up and coming photojournalist and wildlife photographer from Western Australia who by the age of 41 has travelled the world, from the mountains of Nepal, to the deserts of Oman, taking emotional and breathtaking photos of Nature untampered with by humans. Jacob's inspiration for such photography came from infamous photographers of the natural world such as Karl Blossfeldt and Ansel Adams.
